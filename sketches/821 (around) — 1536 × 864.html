“(around)” treats environment as an art installation. Inspired by Dan Graham (1942–2022) and Do-Ho Suh (b. 1962), the work uses translucent, overlapping volumes to blur the boundary between self and environment. As one looks through shifting layers of color and light, the space becomes a series of visual thresholds rather than fixed walls. This gentle instability invites viewers inward, moving through layers of self."/>
